# **App Name**: GROH Connect

## Core Features:

- Homepage Display: Display key information from groh.ng, such as mission, vision and contact details.
- Dynamic Image Carousel: Dynamic image carousel to showcase children and success stories from the website groh.ng.
- AI-Powered Recommendation Tool: An AI-powered tool for suggesting relevant volunteering opportunities and donation options, tailored to the user's expressed interests.
- Contact Form: Contact form for inquiries, directly connected to the organization's email.
- Secure Donation Portal Link: Integration of a secure donation portal link that redirects to groh.ng.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy green (#4CAF50) to evoke growth and stability.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), offering a warm and nurturing backdrop.
- Accent color: Sunny yellow (#FFEB3B) to highlight calls to action and important information.
- Font pairing: 'Roboto Slab' (serif) for headlines and 'Open Sans' (sans-serif) for body text.
- Use leaf-shaped icons with a solid style in primary and accent colors to indicate positive action/emotion.
- Clean and empathetic layout, prioritizing ease of navigation.
- Gentle slide-in effects for content loading and transitions to enhance user engagement without distraction.
